Innovation and Initiatives

    Mobile Platform on Covid-19

  • A team of four students, Anuron Patgiri (MCA), Injamul Islam(BCA), Sanjiban Roy(CSE), Jayantajit Gogoi(CSE) of the University have launched a website to give information on coronavirus pandemic in the state to its residents and NRIs from the region.
  • The website cum dashboard www.covid19assam.in hosts the tracker of covid-19 infected patients in all the districts along with statistical graphs and many other imperative features. It also encloses some vital features like symptom checker, testing center information etc.
    Innovation/Initiative validated by: Dr. Pranab Das.

  • Dr. Uzzal Sharma, Patent No. 324636-001 on Steering Wheel Locking System
